Welcome to SCHE-Eclipse Robotics

The SCHE-Eclipse Robotics team is competing in the BEST Robotics competition. In addition to developing Simulink code to operate the robot for the competition, the programming team has also been pursuing a few side projects. On these pages, you will find some of these projects developed by the programming team over the past couple of years.

BEST 2013 - Gate Keeper

BEST 2014 - Blade Runner

In 2014, the programming team learned JavaScript and developed an interactive 3D simulation of the "Blade Runner" competition.

BEST 2015 - Pay Dirt

The programming team is taking lessons learned from last year and are once again developing an inteactive 3D simulation of the playfield for the "Pay Dirt" competition.

BEST 2016 - Bet the Farm

For the 2016 contest, the programming team recreated a very complex play field and pre-programmed a demonstration run of the team's robot for the "Bet the Farm" competition.
